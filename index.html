About

Hi, I'm Dom, a self-taught developer from Merseyside, UK.

I started learning to code while working as a teacher in South Korea, and haven't looked back since. I love building things with the same playfulness and creativity that made me popular with my students. My diverse life experiences have given me a unique toolbox to work with, complimenting my ever-growing code knowledge with an array of soft skills and useful perspectives.

Outside of coding, you can find me spending time taking and developing pictures, trying to unearth musical gems from the depths of Spotify and Bandcamp, watching weird old films, learning Korean, or playing with the family dog.
