Nice to meet you!

I'm Dom, a self-taught software developer from Merseyside, UK.

I previously worked as a teacher in South Korea, becoming an effective communicator, even through language and cultural barriers.

Despite being on track for a career in teaching, I had always been curious about other directions, including programming. During a quiet office hour, I took a break from honing my PowerPoint skills and began learning to code.

I quickly realised it was what I wanted to do.

Since then, I've taken the time to learn not only to code, but, without the deadlines of a bootcamp to motivate me, how to remain consistent, plan my own curriculum, and push myself to continue learning effectively.


Outside of coding, you can find me studying Korean, taking and developing pictures, trying to unearth musical gems from the depths of Spotify and Bandcamp, watching weird old films, or playing with the family dog.
